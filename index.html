My First Blog Post

5/13/15

So I will be using this website to blog the process on my new digital card game I intend to make. The setting of the game will be that both players (for right now just two player) are issuing commands to their respective pilots who are piloting giant robots. The games goal is to defeat the opponent using a variety of attacks depending on what weapons are being equipped. Now the point of the game which makes it a card game in the general sense is that you can collect different equipment (such as arms, legs, head piece, core, etc).
